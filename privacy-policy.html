Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 9, 2024
Effective Date: December 9, 2024

Simple Summary: Invoicer is a local application that runs in your browser. We don't collect, store, or share your data. When you connect your Gmail, we only request permission to send emails on your behalf. All your invoice data stays on your computer.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Invoicer ("we," "our," or "the Application"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our invoice generation application.

Invoicer is a client-side web application that helps you create and send professional invoices. The application runs entirely in your web browser and does not transmit your data to any external servers except as explicitly described below.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When using Invoicer, you may enter:

  • Your business name and email address
  • Client names and email addresses
  • Invoice details (numbers, dates, amounts, descriptions)
  • Payment information and notes

Storage: All this information is stored locally in your web browser only. We do not transmit, store, or access this data on any server.

2.2 Gmail API Access

When you choose to connect your Gmail account to send invoices:

  • What we access: Your email address (for authentication purposes only)
  • What we can do: Send emails on your behalf with PDF invoice attachments
  • What we CANNOT do: Read your existing emails, access your contacts, or modify your Gmail settings

Gmail Permissions: We only request the gmail.send scope, which allows the application to send emails. We DO NOT request permission to read your emails or access any other Gmail data.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Local Data Processing

All invoice data you enter is used exclusively to:

  • Generate invoice previews in your browser
  • Create PDF documents locally on your device
  • Pre-fill email messages when sending invoices

3.2 Gmail Integration

When you authorize Gmail access, we use it only to:

  • Authenticate your Google account
  • Send invoice emails with PDF attachments to your clients
  • Display your connected email address in the application

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage Only

All your data stays on your device. Invoicer does not have backend servers or databases. Your invoice information is never uploaded, transmitted, or stored on any external servers.

4.2 OAuth Tokens

When you connect Gmail, Google provides a temporary access token that expires after 1 hour. This token is stored temporarily in your browser's memory and is automatically deleted when you close the application.

4.3 No Cloud Backup

We do not provide cloud backup or synchronization features. If you clear your browser data or use a different device, your invoice information will not be available.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Google OAuth & Gmail API

When you connect your Gmail account, you interact directly with Google's authentication and email services. Google's use of your information is governed by their privacy policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

5.2 CDN Services

The application loads certain libraries (html2pdf.js, Google APIs) from Content Delivery Networks (CDNs). These services may log basic connection information like your IP address and browser type, but they do not receive any of your invoice data.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone.

Your information is never shared with third parties except:

  • Gmail Recipients: When you choose to send an invoice, the email and PDF are sent to the recipient you specify
  • Google: Your email address is shared with Google for authentication purposes only
  • Legal Requirements: Not applicable as we don't store your data

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Gmail Access Control

You can revoke Invoicer's access to your Gmail at any time:

  • Visit Google Account Permissions
  • Find "Invoicer" in the list
  • Click "Remove Access"

7.2 Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally in your browser:

  • Clear your browser cache/data to delete all invoice information
  • Uninstall or stop using the application at any time
  • There is no data stored on our servers to delete

8. Children's Privacy

Invoicer is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised. Continued use of the application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Open Source and Transparency

Invoicer is a simple, transparent application. You can review the source code directly by viewing the HTML file. We believe in full transparency about how your data is handled.

11. Compliance

11.1 Google API Services User Data Policy

Invoicer's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

11.2 Data Minimization

We request the minimum permissions necessary (only gmail.send) and do not access any data beyond what is required to send invoice emails.
